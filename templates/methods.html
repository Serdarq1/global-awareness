Methodology & caveats

Data source

UNODC “Victims of sexual violence” dataset (2013–2022). Values shown as counts and rates per 100,000 population.

Underreporting

Incidents are often underreported. True prevalence may be higher than reported figures.

Comparability

Definitions and reporting systems vary by country. Use regional comparisons with caution.

Updates

We will refresh data when new UNODC releases are available.
